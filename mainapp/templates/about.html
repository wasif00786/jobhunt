Our Story: A Decade-Long Journey

Welcome to Kezan Consulting, where our journey in the world of Human Resource services has been defined by dedication, experience, and a commitment to excellence. Established on January 16, 2023, Kezan Consulting is a unit of Kezan India Private Limited, continuing a legacy that began 12 years ago under the name Saarthi Consulting, operating as a part of Bhumi Enterprises.


With over 12 years of rich industry experience, our journey started as Saarthi Consulting under the umbrella of Bhumi Enterprises. Over the years, we have evolved, grown, and continually refined our expertise to become Kezan Consulting. This transformation reflects our commitment to providing even more comprehensive, innovative, and client-centric Human Resource services
